Volunteer With Us

Your time and skills can rebuild lives. Join our global network of changemakers.

Why Volunteer?

Money buys supplies, but people make change happen. Whether you are a doctor, a designer, a student, or simply someone who cares, there is a role for you in our mission to rebuild Gaza.

Ways to Help

  • Digital Advocacy: Spread the word and help us reach our fundraising goals.
  • Professional Services: Medical, engineering, and psychological support (remote or on-site).
  • Event Support: Help organize local fundraising and awareness events.
