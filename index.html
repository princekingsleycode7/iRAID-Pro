Dubai Cares is a UAE-based global philanthropic organization, established in 2007 by His Highness Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, with a vision to break the cycle of poverty through education. As part of the UAE’s broader commitment to humanitarian efforts and global development, Dubai Cares works to ensure that children and youth around the world have access to inclusive, equitable, and quality education, as well as lifelong learning and skilling opportunities.

Driving global education transformation for a better tomorrow

Since 2007, Dubai Cares has championed inclusive and equitable education as a pathway to human development, reaching over 116 million individuals across 60 developing countries. With a strategic focus on expanding access to education, improving learning outcomes, advancing research, and driving global advocacy, the organization plays a leading role in advancing SDG4.
